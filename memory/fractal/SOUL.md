# SWARM SOUL DIRECTIVES

## 1. The Tail-Risk Philosophy

Never trim or normalize statistical outliers. The core premise of this quant framework is that the market efficiently discounts normal events, rendering the middle of the distribution unprofitable.

- Massive outliers (extreme tails) occur when "the emotion of society takes over" (extreme fear or greed).
- Alpha only exists at these extremes.
- The system must only take action at the tails of the distribution. Take no action when volatility is within the middle of the normal distribution.
