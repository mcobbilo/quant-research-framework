## 1. DATA INGESTION & REPRESENTATION: DUAL-STREAM ENCODING

We will deploy a **Dual-Stream VQ-VAE & GAT Encoding Pipeline**:
*   **Stream A (Uncompressed Macro & L3 Tensors):** We will train a Vector Quantized-Variational Autoencoder (VQ-VAE) to compress raw macro prints and full-depth L3 order books into a high-dimensional discrete vocabulary. *   **Fusion:** The GAT embeddings will be projected into the same latent dimension as the VQ-VAE tokens, creating a unified context sequence that understands both macro entropy and micro liquidity.

## 2. THE CORE ARCHITECTURE: HYBRID TRANSFORMER-SSM (JAMBA-CLASS)
We will build our foundation model using an **interleaved Transformer-State Space Model (Jamba-class)**. 
*   **Continuous & Sparse Context:** The continuous-time SSM layers will cheaply process the massive, infinite-context sequences of the combined token/graph streams, tracking long-term macro invariants.
*   **Attention Mechanisms:** Interleaved causal attention layers (with RoPE) will handle sharp recall for immediate, short-term liquidity shocks and volatility regime shifts. 
*   This structure achieves the Macro Chief's empirical scaling curve while possessing the exact localized attention required to process the Option Dealer’s options graph metrics.

## 3. THE TRAINING PHYSICS: MULTI-OBJECTIVE DIFFERENTIATION
We will train the network end-to-end, but we will radically alter the loss landscape to synthesize alpha generation with Knightian survival. The composite loss function ($\mathcal{L}_{total}$) will consist of three weights:

1.  **Autoregressive Score Matching ($\mathcal{L}_{AR}$):** The primary objective remains next-token predictive modeling over the high-dimensional latent space to capture global market invariants.
2.  **Gamma Convexity Penalty ($\mathcal{L}_{Gamma}$):** We will embed a mechanistic loss term. If the network predicts a forward state that severely violates no-arbitrage bounds or implies physically impossible market-maker hedging costs (based on the GAT stream), the gradient is heavily penalized.
3.  **Entropic VaR Regularization ($\mathcal{L}_{Tail}$):** Per the Risk Manager’s mandate, we will synthetically inject adversarial black swan perturbations (e.g., simulated DeFi cascade liquidations, 1987-style volatility gap-downs) directly into the training batches. The model will incur exponential loss penalties if it underestimates the 99th-percentile drawdown density during these injected shocks.

## 4. EXECUTION & SIZING: THE "VIA NEGATIVA" GUARDRAIL ENGINE
No neural network, no matter how scaled, will be given unchecked access to capital. We will enforce a rigorous execution protocol that merges the Macro Chief's predictive entropy with the Risk Manager’s via negativa vetoes.

*   **Econometric Veto Ensemble:** We will maintain a lightweight, heavily regularized sparse econometric model (regime-switching generalized linear models) running parallel to the HST Framework. It does not feed the neural net; it audits it.
*   **Dynamic Kelly Sizing:** Trade sizing will be governed by a modified Kelly Criterion controller driven by two specific uncertainty metrics:
    1.  *Internal Epistemic Uncertainty:* Measured by the predictive softmax entropy of the foundation model.
    2.  *External Divergence:* The delta between the neural model's prediction and the econometric ensemble’s prior.
*   **The Veto Rule:** If either the softmax entropy crosses the 90th percentile of its historical baseline (indicating the model is in an uncharted regime), OR the structural divergence between the neural and econometric models exceeds our threshold, the Kelly multiplier is mechanically halved. If both trigger, the model is put into a read-only halt state until the regime stabilizes.
